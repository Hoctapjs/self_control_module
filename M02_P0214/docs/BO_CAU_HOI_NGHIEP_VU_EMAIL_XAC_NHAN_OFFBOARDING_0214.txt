BO CAU HOI NGHIEP VU - EMAIL KEM LINK XAC NHAN CONG VIEC OFFBOARDING 0214

Yeu cau dau vao:
"Trong quy trinh 0214, khi quy trinh nghi viec duoc khoi tao, tu dong gui email kem link xac nhan cong viec cho tung bo phan lien quan (IT, Admin, HR)."

Ngu canh module hien tai:
- Module: M02_P0214_QUY_TRINH_OFFBOARDING_RST.
- Model nghiep vu chinh dang dung: approval.request.
- Khi nhan vien submit portal, he thong tao approval.request va action_confirm().
- Sau khi approval duoc phe duyet, action_approve() hien dang tao checklist offboarding tu offboarding_activity_plan_rst.
- Checklist hien co co cac dau viec IT, Admin, HR duoi dang mail.activity.
- Hien da co cau hinh default IT/Admin/HR user tren res.company.
- Hien da co email template nhac viec bo phan, nhung chua ro yeu cau email "link xac nhan cong viec" ngay luc khoi tao.

Muc tieu cua bo cau hoi:
- Lam ro trigger gui email.
- Lam ro nguoi nhan va cach xac dinh bo phan phu trach.
- Lam ro link xac nhan, quyen truy cap, bao mat, trang thai va audit trail.
- Lam ro noi dung email, reminder, loi va tieu chi nghiem thu.

13. Tieu chi nghiem thu

88. Vui long xac nhan acceptance criteria toi thieu:
    - Khi workflow den dung trigger, he thong tao/gia han checklist cho IT/Admin/HR.
    - Moi bo phan nhan email dung nguoi nhan.
    - Email co link xac nhan rieng theo activity/bo phan.
    - Bam link hop le se danh dau cong viec tuong ung da hoan thanh.
    - Chatter/audit co ghi nhan nguoi xac nhan va thoi diem.
    - Quy trinh chi hoan tat khi cac dieu kien bat buoc da xong.
